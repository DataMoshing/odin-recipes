Cereal Milk

Description

In this recipe i will show you how to make the infamous cereal milk.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of corn flakes
  • 2 cups of cold milk (You can use oat milk or almond milk)
  • 4 tbsp of sugar

Steps

  1. Pour corn flakes into milk in a very large and comical mug.
  2. Place cereal and milk in fridge for about 1 hr.
  3. Strain cereal milk so you're only left with milk, discard the soggy cereal unless you want a snack.
  4. Mix in 4 tbsp of sugar.
  5. ENJOY.
