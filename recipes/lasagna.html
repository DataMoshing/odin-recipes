Lasagna Recipe

Description

In this recipe I will show you how to make the most mouth-watering and BEST lasagna you will ever taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of ground beef
  • 1/2 cup of onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 (32 oz) can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 2 tbsp of tomato paste
  • 20 lasagna sheets
  • 1 lb of mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup of parmesan cheese
  • 1 tbsp of dried basil

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 420 degrees F.
  2. In a large pan or Dutch cook ground beef,onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned. Mix in the can of crushed tomatoes,tomato paste, and water.Season with 1 tbsp of each of salt,black pepper, sugar, and dried basil. Simmer, covered, for about 30 min, stirring frequently.
  3. Bring a large pot of really salted water to a rolling boil.Cook the lasagna sheets in the boiling water for about 7-8 min or until al dente.Drain the noodles in a colander and set aside.
  4. In a large deep dish pan assemble meat sauce, pasta, and mozzarella cheese. Rinse and repeat. (Don't actually rinse anything,just repeat)
  5. Finally top it with parmesan cheese and cover with aluminum foil.
  6. Bake in oven for about 20 min.Remove foil and bake for another 15 min.Then turn on broiler and set the lasagna on the top rack for 1-3 min until bubbling.
  7. Bone apple tea.
